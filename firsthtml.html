Tracking Time

The theme of 2018 for me is being more self-aware of where my attention is going towards.
It’s incorporating in elements of minimalism, as well as a multitude of self-help books and different podcast sources that I now don’t remember.
I’ve been using an app called Hours, in which I try and track the usage of my time in 5 categories:

Extracurriculars

Health

Studying

Assignment Work

Class

Week Health Studying Assignment Work Class Extracurriculars Total
March 18-24 1:34 1:25 7:18 8:37 14:20 33:13
March 25-31 2:32 0:44 27:00 7:08 14:39 52:04

The intended goal is for this to be a way for me to see whether or not I am balancing different aspects of my life well enough or not. Many times, I find that it is extremely easy to be busy, but not really be busy with the things that are truly important.
I like to think of it as a more deliberate lifestyle, where I don’t let what’s in front of me dictate what I spend my time on, but rather what I truly believe are worthwhile pursuits.
